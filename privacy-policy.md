# AudioThrough Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 12, 2026  
**Last updated:** June 12, 2026

Drew Nichols (“we,” “us,” or “our”) built AudioThrough as a local macOS utility for routing audio between input and output devices. This policy describes how the app handles information on your Mac.

## Summary

AudioThrough does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. The app works entirely on your device and does not connect to the internet.

## Information the app accesses

### Audio input (microphone permission)

macOS may ask you to allow microphone access. AudioThrough uses this permission only to receive audio from the **input device you select** in settings (for example, a loopback or virtual audio device). Audio is processed locally for routing to your chosen outputs. Audio is **not** recorded to disk, uploaded, or sent to us or any third party.

### Audio output

AudioThrough sends audio to output devices you choose. Output routing happens locally on your Mac.

### Settings and configuration

AudioThrough stores your configurations, mappings, channel matrices, volumes, and related editor settings as a JSON file in the app’s sandboxed Application Support folder on your Mac. This data stays on your device. We do not have access to it.

### Optional launch at login

If you enable **Launch at login**, macOS registers a small login helper that starts AudioThrough when you sign in. This feature does not send data off your Mac.

## Information we do not collect

AudioThrough does **not**:

- connect to the internet or use network APIs
- include analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs
- create an account or require sign-in
- access your contacts, photos, files, browsing history, or location
- use encryption to transmit data (there is no transmission)

## Third parties

AudioThrough does not share data with third parties because it does not collect or transmit data.

## Children’s privacy

AudioThrough is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

## Data retention and deletion

All app data remains on your Mac until you delete it. You can remove AudioThrough’s saved settings by deleting the app and its data from Application Support, or by resetting configurations inside the app.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
